About me

I’m an easy going hard working designer with over 10 years experience. Having first started out as a graphic designer working in print, I moved into digital approximately 5 years ago and have since fallen in love with all things digital including animation where my current focus lies. I have plenty to contribute with a thirst to continue learning.

I’ve also worked on web design projects such as designing and building out landing pages, UI components & other web related content.

Please view my portfolio below and If you’d like to get in touch, please shoot me an email or give me a call.

Design

Skilled in Adobe CC software such as Photoshop, Illustrator, XD and After Effects. Mainly used for creating web and social content including landing pages, storyboards and animations.

Animation

Skills in storyboarding and motion graphics using After Effects as my main animation tool. Projects include animating content for social use such as weekly special sales, logo intros and campaign related messaging for things like Christmas, Easter, Black Friday to name a few.

Web

Skills include HTML, CSS and Javascript. I also have experience using Wordpress and other custom CMS platforms to build out landing pages and/or EDMs.
