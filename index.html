About me

If you need a creative spark to join your team, then I'm your man! As an experienced Graphic Designer, I have always been a creative thinker. With over 8 years experience I have mainly worked within the print and publishing sector. Anything from brochures and catalogues to packaging, POS material and signage; if it needs printing I've got you covered.

I also have skills and interests in other areas such as Web design, Video editing and 3D visualisation. I believe in keeping ahead of the times and for this reason have delved into these technologies to strengthen myself as a designer.

Please view my samples and if you'd like to get in touch, just shoot me an email or give me a call. Cheers.

Print

Advanced skills using various Adobe CC software including Photoshop, Illustrator and Indesign. From manipulating images in Photoshop to creating something from nothing in Illustrator and bringing it all together in Indesign.

Web

Skills include HTML, CSS and Javascript. I also have experience with jQuery, Ruby on Rails, XML and Git and am continuously experimenting with other technologies all the time. I like to play around with Bootstrap and also create EDMs as well as HTML5 web banners.

Video

Skills include editing and enhancing video footage using Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro. From adding background music to animated branded intros and slides, I can take your raw footage and turn it into a professional corporate video.

3D

My 3D software of choice is The Foundry's 'Modo' however I also have experience using Maya and Cinema 4D. From creating stills for an ad, visualising a product concept or sending a model away for 3D printing. 3D is proving more useful and versatile then ever before.
