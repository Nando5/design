About me

Hi there! I am an easy going hard working designer with over 8 years experience. I have worked as a digital designer for 4 years mainly in web and before that worked in print creating things like brochures, packaging and many other POS material. I have a keen eye for design with plenty to contribute and a thirst to learn more.

I have worked on various web related projects such as designing and coding landing pages with HTML, CSS and some JS, designing and building EDMs in platforms such as Hubspot aswell as making updates to company websites as needed while keeping to company styleguides or otherwise creating branding such as font and colour for individual clients.

Please view samples of my design and coding skills. If you'd like to get in touch, just shoot me an email or give me a call. Cheers.

Design

Many years experience using Adobe CC software such as Photoshop, Illustrator & Indesign. Skills also include Sketch and XD for designing and prototyping landing pages, EDMs or any other web related content.

Web

Skills include HTML, CSS, Javascript, Jquery and Bootstrap. I also have experience using Wordpress and other custom CMS platforms to build out landing pages as well as Hubspot to build out EDMs and/or landing pages.

Video

Skills include editing and enhancing video footage using Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro. Animation skills include things like lower thirds as well as logo intros.
