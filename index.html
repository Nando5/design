About me

4 years experience working as a digital designer mainly in web as well as some animation. I am an easy going, hard working designer with plenty to contribute and a thirst to learn more.

I have worked on various web related projects such as designing and coding landing pages with HTML, CSS and some JS, designing and building EDMs in platforms such as Hubspot aswell as making updates to company websites as needed while keeping to company styleguides or otherwise creating branding such as font and colour for individual clients.

Please view samples of my design and coding skills. If you'd like to get in touch, just shoot me an email or give me a call. Cheers.

Design

Advanced skills using various Adobe CC software including Photoshop & Illustrator. Skills also include Sketch and XD for designing and prototyping landing pages, EDMs or any other web related content.

Web

Skills include HTML, CSS, Javascript, Jquery and Bootstrap. I also have experience with Wordpress and other custom CMS platforms to build out landing pages as well as Hubspot to build out EDMs and/or landing pages.
